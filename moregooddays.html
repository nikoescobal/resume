Pre-Arrival Prospecting Pipeline — Hotel Sales Ops Claude Code · Puppeteer · Clay

Biweekly pipeline for a multi-brand hospitality operator: scrapes a Citrix-hosted arrivals portal (HTML5 canvas) via Puppeteer + screen capture, extracts the arrivals grid with Claude Code, runs deterministic filters (length-of-stay caps, rate-plan exclusions, dedup), enriches guests via Clay, and cross-references Agency360 and Lighthouse data to tier each lead (corporate accounts vs. declining segments vs. flagged). Emails a tiered HTML prospect report to the sales team with a double-confirm guardrail and full audit log. The high-value pieces: reliable portal extraction and the Agency360/Lighthouse tiering — those are what turn a raw arrivals list into ranked outreach.
